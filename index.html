Introducing InfluViz

InfluViz is the groundbreaking application designed to revolutionize the live music experience. It bridges the gap between the audience and the stage, transforming passive viewers into active participants. As a music promoter or visual effects producer, you can now harness the power of the crowd to create immersive, interactive events that captivate and energize.

How It Works

InfluViz allows your audience to influence the visual effects in real-time through their smartphones. When they move their phones, the app sends this data to your sound technician, who can seamlessly integrate these movements into the visual spectacle. This dynamic interaction is not only engaging but also creates a unique, unforgettable experience for every attendee.

Seamless Integration

InfluViz is designed for compatibility and ease of use. It integrates effortlessly with industry-standard tools such as TouchDesigner, Max/MSP, and Ableton Live, using the powerful OpenSoundControl (OSC) protocol. This means you can:

  • Create Mesmerizing Visuals: Sync audience movements with stunning visuals in TouchDesigner.
  • Dynamic Soundscapes: Use Max/MSP to generate audio-reactive visuals and effects that respond to the crowd's energy.
  • Live Performances: Enhance live sets in Ableton Live by incorporating real-time audience feedback.
